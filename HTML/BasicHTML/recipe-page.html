My Favorite Recipe - Grilled Cheese

One of my favorite recipes is Grilled Cheese. It just gives that down-home taste when I need some comfort.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your favorite bread
  • 2-4 slices of your favorite cheese(s)
  • mayonnaise
  • butter

Instructions

  1. Start by spreading mayonnaise lightly on the insides of the bread slices.
  2. Next, place the slices of cheese between the bread.
  3. Preheat a small frying pan or griddle over medium heat.
  4. Spread butter on one side of your sandwich, then place butter in the preheated pan and let it melt.
  5. Place the sandwich in the pan with the unbuttered side in the melted butter.
  6. Grill the sandwich until the bread is golden brown, then flip the sandwich over to grill the other side - about 3 minutes per side.
  7. Remove the sandwich from the pan and serve on a plate, cut in half. If desired, serve with a bowl or mug of Cream of Tomato soup.
